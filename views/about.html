Victoria Erickson


She graduated from Savannah College of Art and Design in 2014 with a B.F.A. in Graphic Design and two minors in Advertising and Photography. Victoria is currently pursuing a career as a UX UI Designer in Manhattan, New York and she currently resides in Queens, New York. Along with her work for the web, she does freelance graphic design work to stay up to date on the latest adobe techniques. She has taken night classes at New York Code and Design Academy to earn certificates in front end development and web development to help her at work. She wants to become a well-rounded specialist within her field by understanding both front and back end development.

In her free time, she enjoys watching tv and anime, reading manga, playing video games, hanging out with friends, and going to the gym.
